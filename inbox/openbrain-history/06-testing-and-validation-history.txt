v0.5.1 and v0.5.2 repeated-cycle validation

The project started validating repeated ingest-to-assistant behavior rather than only single runs.

What was tested:
- invalid ingest rejection
- valid ingest success
- duplicate replay rejection
- trace propagation
- suspect-row exclusion
- latency drift and noise trends across bounded repeated cycles

What passed:
Supported by the script pass message:
Tracing, duplicate protection, invalid rejection, and suspect-row filtering were expected to hold across repeated cycles.

What still needed verification:
Confirmed by docs/crispybrain-v0_5_2.md:
Longer cycle counts mostly reconfirm stability unless they reveal new failures or suspect rows.
